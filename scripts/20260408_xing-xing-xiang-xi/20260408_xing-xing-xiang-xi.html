中文 大家好，欢迎来到小雪的中文屋。我是小雪，Snowy。
拼音 dàjiā hǎo, huānyíng lái dào xiǎo xuě de zhōngwén wū. wǒ shì Xiǎo Xuě, Snowy.

拼音 nǐ yǒu méiyǒu yù dào guò zhèyàng de rén——dì yī cì jiànmiàn, què gǎnjué xiàng rènshi le hěn jiǔ?

② 成语引出

中文 看完了片段，大家猜到今天的成语了吗？对，就是——惺惺相惜。惺惺相惜。惺惺相惜。
拼音 kàn wán le piànduàn, dàjiā cāi dào jīntiān de chéngyǔ le ma? duì, jiù shì——
영어 After watching, did everyone guess today's idiom? That's right — 惺惺相惜.
中文 我们还是先来分析一下每一个字的意思。
拼音 wǒmen háishi xiān lái fēnxī yīxià měi yīgè zì de yìsi.
영어 Let's start by analysing the meaning of each character.

③ 字义分析

【惺 · xīng】

中文 "惺"，左边是心字旁，右边是"星"。心里像星星一样明亮，这个字的意思是聪明、清醒、明辨是非。
拼音 "Xīng", zuǒbiān shì xīnzìpáng, yòubiān shì "xīng". xīnlǐ xiàng xīngxīng yīyàng míngliàng, zhège zì de yìsi shì cōngmíng, qīngxǐng, míngbiàn shìfēi.
영어 "Xīng" has the heart radical on the left and "star" (星) on the right — bright like a star in the mind. It means intelligent, clear-headed, and perceptive.
中文 比如"睡眼惺忪"，形容刚睡醒、眼睛还没完全睁开的样子。
拼音 bǐrú "shuìyǎn xīng sōng", xíngróng gāng shuì xǐng, yǎnjīng hái méi wánquán zhēng kāi de yàngzi.
영어 For example, 睡眼惺忪 describes the drowsy, half-open eyes of someone who has just woken up.
中文 "惺惺"两字叠用，则指聪明、有见识的人，或彼此心思相通的状态。
拼音 "xīng xīng" liǎng zì dié yòng, zé zhǐ cōngmíng, yǒu jiànshi de rén, huò bǐcǐ xīnsī xiāngtōng de zhuàngtài.
영어 When doubled as 惺惺, it refers to perceptive, insightful people — or a state of mutual understanding between them.

【相 · xiāng】

中文 "相"在这里读第一声，表示"互相、彼此"的意思。
拼音 "Xiāng" zài zhèlǐ dú dì yī shēng, biǎoshì "hùxiāng, bǐcǐ" de yìsi.
영어 "Xiāng" is read in the first tone here, meaning "mutually" or "each other".
中文 相关的词有"相互"、"相信"、"相遇"——都带有双方之间发生关系的意思。
拼音 xiāngguān de cí yǒu "xiānghù", "xiāngxìn", "xiāngyù"——dōu dài yǒu shuāngfāng zhījiān fāshēng guānxi de yìsi.
영어 Related words: 相互 (mutual), 相信 (to trust), 相遇 (to meet) — all involving a relationship between two parties.

【惜 · xī】

中文 "惜"的意思是珍视、爱惜，觉得某件事或某个人很宝贵、舍不得失去。
拼音 "Xī" de yìsi shì zhēnshì, àixī, juéde mǒu jiàn shì huò mǒu gè rén hěn bǎoguì, shě bu de shīqù.
영어 "Xī" means to cherish or treasure — to feel that something or someone is precious and not want to lose it.
中文 比如"珍惜时间"、"爱惜生命"，还有"可惜"，表示遗憾、感到惋惜。
拼音 bǐrú "zhēnxī shíjiān", "àixī shēngmìng", hái yǒu "kěxī", biǎoshì yíhàn, gǎndào wǎnxī.
영어 Examples: 珍惜时间 (cherish time), 爱惜生命 (value life), and 可惜 (what a pity).

④ 成语整体含义

中文 "惺惺相惜"，字面意思是：聪明的人互相珍视对方。
拼音 "Xīng xīng xiāng xī", zìmiàn yìsi shì: cōngmíng de rén hùxiāng zhēnshì duìfāng.
영어 The literal meaning of 惺惺相惜 is: intelligent people cherish and value each other.
中文 引申开来，是指性格、才华或经历相似的人，能够互相理解、彼此欣赏。
拼音 yǐnshēn kāi lái, shì zhǐ xìnggé, cáihuá huò jīnglì xiāngsì de rén, nénggòu hùxiāng lǐjiě, bǐcǐ xīnshǎng.
영어 More broadly, it describes people of similar character, talent, or experience who understand each other deeply and mutually appreciate one another.
中文 这种感觉不一定来自长久的认识，有时第一次见面就会有这种心灵相通的感受。
拼音 zhè zhǒng gǎnjué bù yīdìng lái zì chángjiǔ de rènshi, yǒushí dì yī cì jiànmiàn jiù huì yǒu zhè zhǒng xīnlíng xiāngtōng de gǎnshòu.
영어 This feeling doesn't require long acquaintance — sometimes you sense it the very first time you meet.
中文 近义词有"志同道合"，强调共同的目标和理念；还有"一见如故"，形容初次见面就像老朋友。
拼音 jìnyìcí yǒu "zhì tóng dào hé", qiángdiào gòngtóng de mùbiāo hé lǐniàn; hái yǒu "yījiàn rúgù", xíngróng chūcì jiànmiàn jiù xiàng lǎo péngyou.
영어 Near-synonyms: 志同道合 (like-minded, sharing goals) and 一见如故 (feeling like old friends at first meeting).

⑤ 成语来源 — 源自古谚 · "惺惺惜惺惺，好汉惜好汉"

中文 古语有云："惺惺惜惺惺，好汉惜好汉。"
拼音 gǔyǔ yǒu yún: "xīng xīng xī xīng xīng, hǎohàn xī hǎohàn."
영어 An old saying goes: "The perceptive cherish the perceptive; the bold cherish the bold."
中文 这句话的意思是：聪明的人能看见另一个聪明人的价值，英雄能理解另一个英雄的不易。
拼音 zhè jù huà de yìsi shì: cōngmíng de rén néng kànjiàn lìng yīgè cōngmíng rén de jiàzhí, yīngxióng néng lǐjiě lìng yīgè yīngxióng de bùyì.
영어 Its meaning: an intelligent person can recognize the value in another intelligent person; a hero understands the hardships of another hero.
中文 后来，"惺惺惜惺惺"简化为"惺惺相惜"，在文学作品中广泛流传，专门形容同类之间那种无需言说的默契与珍视。
拼音 hòulái, "xīng xīng xī xīng xīng" jiǎnhuà wéi "xīng xīng xiāng xī", zài wénxué zuòpǐn zhōng guǎngfàn liúchuán, zhuānmén xíngróng tónglèi zhījiān nà zhǒng wú xū yán shuō de mòqì yǔ zhēnshì.
영어 Over time, 惺惺惜惺惺 was condensed into 惺惺相惜, spreading widely in literature to describe the unspoken understanding and mutual appreciation between kindred spirits.

⑥ 例句与片段回顾

例句 1

中文 两位作家第一次见面，就聊了整整一夜，果然是惺惺相惜。
拼音 liǎng wèi zuòjiā dì yī cì jiànmiàn, jiù liáo le zhěngzhěng yī yè, guǒrán shì xīng xīng xiāng xī.
영어 The two writers met for the first time and ended up talking all night — true kindred spirits indeed.
中文 这句话说的是：两个有才华的人相遇，不需要太多时间，就能感受到彼此的共鸣。
拼音 zhè jù huà shuō de shì: liǎng gè yǒu cáihuá de rén xiāngyù, bù xūyào tài duō shíjiān, jiù néng gǎnshòu dào bǐcǐ de gòngmíng.
영어 This shows that two talented people, upon meeting, quickly feel a deep resonance with each other.

例句 2

中文 他们虽然是竞争对手，却惺惺相惜，彼此都十分欣赏对方的实力。
拼音 tāmen suīrán shì jìngzhēng duìshǒu, què xīng xīng xiāng xī, bǐcǐ dōu shífēn xīnshǎng duìfāng de shílì.
영어 Even though they were rivals, they held a deep mutual admiration — each greatly respecting the other's abilities.
中文 这里很有意思——即使是竞争对手，也可以惺惺相惜。这个词强调的是欣赏，而不是亲密的友谊。
拼音 zhèlǐ hěn yǒuyìsi——jíshǐ shì jìngzhēng duìshǒu, yě kěyǐ xīng xīng xiāng xī. zhège cí qiángdiào de shì xīnshǎng, ér bùshì qīnmì de yǒuyì.
영어 Notably, even rivals can 惺惺相惜. The phrase is about appreciation — not necessarily close friendship.

拼音 nà wǒmen huítóu zài lái kàn yīxià kāitóu de yǐngshì piànduàn——

拼音 dāng juésè shuō chū "xīng xīng xiāng xī", biǎodá de shì yī zhǒng shēnshēn de lǐjiě yǔ zhēnshì——jíshǐ liǎng rén xìnggé bùtóng, zài nèixīn shēnchù què shì tóngyī lèi rén.
영어 When the character says 惺惺相惜, they express deep understanding and regard — even if the two are different on the surface, at heart they are the same kind of person.

⑥-B 填空练习

Q1. 她和新认识的同事聊了几句，发现彼此想法极为相似，真是________。

After a short chat with her new colleague, she found they thought alike — they were truly ________.

✅ 她和新认识的同事聊了几句，发现彼此想法极为相似，真是惺惺相惜。

After a short chat with her new colleague, she found they thought alike — they were truly kindred spirits.

Q2. 两位老艺术家虽然初次见面，却________，相谈甚欢。

Though the two veteran artists met for the first time, they ________ and got along wonderfully.

✅ 两位老艺术家虽然初次见面，却惺惺相惜，相谈甚欢。

Though the two veteran artists met for the first time, they recognized each other as kindred spirits and got along wonderfully.

Q3. 他们是对手，但比赛结束后的那次握手，让人感受到了一种________。

They were rivals, but that handshake after the match gave everyone a sense of ________.

✅ 他们是对手，但比赛结束后的那次握手，让人感受到了一种惺惺相惜。

They were rivals, but that handshake after the match gave everyone a sense of deep mutual admiration.

⑦ 使用注意

中文 "惺惺相惜"用来形容两个或两个以上的人之间的关系，不能用来形容一个人。
拼音 "xīng xīng xiāng xī" yòng lái xíngróng liǎng gè huò liǎng gè yǐshàng de rén zhījiān de guānxi, bù néng yòng lái xíngróng yī gè rén.
영어 惺惺相惜 describes a relationship between two or more people — it cannot describe a single person alone.
中文 这个词带有正面、温暖的感情色彩，通常用于有才能、有品格、或经历相似的人之间。
拼音 zhège cí dài yǒu zhèngmiàn, wēnnuǎn de qínggǎn sècǎi, tōngcháng yòng yú yǒu cáinéng, yǒu pǐngé, huò jīnglì xiāngsì de rén zhījiān.
영어 It carries a positive, warm emotional tone, typically used between people of talent, character, or similar life experience.
中文 也可以用于对手之间——比如两位棋手或运动员，互相欣赏对方的实力，感情上不一定亲密。
拼音 yě kěyǐ yòng yú duìshǒu zhījiān——bǐrú liǎng wèi qíshǒu huò yùndòngyuán, hùxiāng xīnshǎng duìfāng de shílì, qínggǎn shàng bù yīdìng qīnmì.
영어 It can also apply to rivals — for instance, two chess players or athletes who admire each other's skill, even without being close friends.
